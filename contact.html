Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the minimum stay requirement?
The minimum stay at Marina Glen Unit 51 is 2 nights. During peak season (December - January), we may require a minimum of 5 nights.
Is parking available?
Yes! We offer free on-site private parking with 24/7 security for all guests. One parking space is included with your booking.
Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed at Marina Glen Unit 51 to maintain the comfort of all our guests.
Is WiFi included?
WiFi is not included, but guests can arrange mobile data or use nearby facilities.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 14 days before arrival. Cancellations within 14 days may incur a fee. Please contact us for specific details.
How far is the nearest beach?
Marina Beach is within walking distance, and the beautiful Southbroom Beach is just 1.8 km away - about a 5-minute drive.
