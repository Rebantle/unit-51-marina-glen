Nearby Beaches

Discover pristine beaches just minutes from Marina Glen Unit 51

Your Gateway to Paradise

Marina Glen Unit 51 is perfectly located near some of South Africa's most beautiful beaches. Whether you're looking to relax on golden sands, swim in crystal-clear waters, or enjoy exciting water sports, our prime location offers easy access to stunning coastal destinations.

Southbroom Beach

1.8 km away

The closest beach to Marina Glen, Southbroom Beach is a pristine stretch of golden sand perfect for families, swimmers, and sunbathers. Known for its clean waters and safe swimming conditions.

Safe Swimming Family Friendly Facilities Parking

Marina Beach

Walking distance

Right at your doorstep! Marina Beach offers a quiet, intimate setting perfect for early morning walks, sunset views, and peaceful relaxation away from the crowds.

Walking Distance Sunset Views Rock Pools Quiet

Margate Main Beach

15 km away

A popular beach destination with vibrant atmosphere, water sports activities, and beachfront restaurants. Perfect for those seeking more action and entertainment.

Water Sports Restaurants Shops Lifeguards

San Lameer Beach

3 km away

An exclusive beach within the San Lameer Resort. Known for its pristine conditions, excellent swimming, and beautiful natural surroundings. A hidden gem for beach lovers.

Exclusive Great Swimming Natural Beauty Scenic
